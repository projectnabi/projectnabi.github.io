Get started on your personal projects

Getting started on a personal project without deadlines can be difficult. Early Bird makes it easy to get started on your projects and turn them into habits, through a gamified experience that rewards productivity and makes large, daunting tasks more approachable.

Start with 2 minutes

Get started on a project by committing to working on it for 2 minutes. Keep going past 2 minutes if you get into the flow, or stop if you're not feeling it.

Take care of birds

Work on your projects to hatch eggs into a variety of birds, then keep them fed and happy.

Streaks and stats

Keep track of how much time you've worked on projects, and work consistently to keep your streaks going.

Cognitive behavioral therapy

Early Bird uses techniques from cognitive behavioral therapy to restructure your perceptions of your personal projects, making them feel easier to start and more rewarding to make progress on.
